Upholstery Cleaning

Upholstery Cleaning Services

At Clean and Shine we provide Carpets and Upholstery cleaning service which are:

  • Sofas
  • Dining chairs
  • Armchairs
  • Office chairs
  • Couches
  • Mattres
  • Office carpet cleaning
  • Residential wall to wall carpets and rug cleaning

This service is suitable for carpets in high traffic areas, to remove most stains on upholstery or for an annual clean up to reduce the allergies caused by dirty carpets or upholstery. Our team well trained to use our carpet cleaning machines.

Not only that they can find ways to enhance the performance and the end results of the carpet cleaning by using certain products for stains. They really take the time to understand how to deliver maximum carpet & upholstery cleaning results. We are using the best machines to deliver a quality carpet & upholstery cleaning service.

The method used to clean Rugs & Fabric Upholstery

  • Thorough vacuuming with an electronic Vacuum cleaner
  • Prespray with pH Neutral Detergents
  • Brush in with contra rotating brushes
  • Work on the spots and stains using spot removers and hand brush
* Some stains over time become permanent hence we cannot guarantee complete removal of all types of stains.
