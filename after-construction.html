Post Construction Cleaning

Complete Building Cleaning Services to Suit Every Need

Residential Cleaning Services in Dubai

Construction sites are often filled with debris and dust due to all the work that is being done. Cement and other building materials are streamed in on a daily basis to finish the project. People and equipment cause tremendous indoor traffic regularly, as work gets done. As such, it comes as no surprise that a construction site is littered and strewn with all kinds of rubble at the end of a project.

Dubai’s Best Choice for Post Construction Cleaning Services

There is a lot of work that needs to be done in order to ensure that a building is fit for tenancy. The government of Dubai is extremely particular when it comes to quality of the indoor environment of a building. In fact, the government has a list of requirements specifically directed at the maintenance of construction sites after the completion of a project.

Make sure that your project site is up to local government standards with the expertise of Clean & Shine. Drawing on years of experience in building maintenance, we will work to achieve an intensive kind of clean for any location. Whether you are in need of building cleaning services for the after construction stage, or regular rubble hauling for an ongoing project, we are here to help.

We have the equipment and the manpower to take on any kind of order. Whether you are working on a high-class hotel or an affordable and accessible apartment for young professionals, we are perfect for the job.

Intensive Post Construction Cleaning for a Complete Overall Clean

As part of the after construction cleaning process, we will transport any building or demolition waste to designated disposal sites per government regulations. To keep the refuse manageable, we will also conduct the necessary measures to quarantine the debris within a single location for transport.
